About Me...

My life is full of adventures that occur in my day to day, I am very dynamic and sports fan; basketball and soccer have helped make more friends and connect with people so learn from them and contribute from every interaction, in addition to the importance of working as a team and understanding that everything can be achieved and excellent results can be obtained.

I do not fear challenges, they excite and inspire me to reach the impossible; that's why I climb mountains and enjoy the path to reach the top and experience that mixture of adrenaline and excitement they produce.

Entering the cockpit of an airplane when I was little was a great achievement, that little moment for me was magical and I left the with great joy of knowing that nothing is impossible and I will always be and achieve what I want and go for the good of all. Also, since that moment a passion arise to with outer space creatures which has led to a desire to discover as well as start my own company that does missions and studies outer space.

Scouts have enriched me with personal, social, cultural, spiritual values ​​such as caring for the environment through reforestation days, to help people and give them, being a scout has made me an independent, altruistic, compassionate and generous character with life and the people around me.

I am part of a Robotics team, is something that fascinates me and transports me to another dimension of dreams to achieve, of innovation, creativity, imagination with all the possibility of making it a reality with all the possibilities of creating machines that are of help and great benefit for humanity and thus create a permanent difference that transcends in time.

I have been part of several leadership groups and meritorious student which fills me with pride and joy because in a way or other I contribute to excellence and create precedents that others can follow and be that positive example to others.
